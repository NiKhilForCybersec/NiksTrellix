Company History & Timeline

The evolution from McAfee and FireEye to Trellix.

Reference üìñ 10 min read

Timeline

1987 - McAfee Associates Founded
John McAfee founds McAfee Associates, pioneering commercial antivirus software.
2004 - FireEye Founded
Ashar Aziz founds FireEye, focusing on advanced threat detection.
2011 - Intel Acquires McAfee
Intel acquires McAfee for $7.68 billion. Products rebranded to Intel Security.
2014 - FireEye Acquires Mandiant
FireEye acquires Mandiant for $1 billion, gaining world-class incident response.
2016 - McAfee Spins Out
TPG acquires majority stake from Intel. McAfee brand returns.
2021 - McAfee Enterprise + FireEye Merge
Symphony Technology Group acquires McAfee Enterprise and FireEye products.
2022 - Trellix Launches
Combined company rebrands as Trellix, focusing on XDR. Mandiant sold to Google.
